Privacy & Safety

Built for a world where
discretion is the default.

Fifth Circle was designed from the ground up for residents who value privacy. Every data decision — what's collected, who sees it, when it's shared — is resident-controlled and explicitly consented.

◎
No public directory

Residents are never visible to each other without a curated introduction. No profiles to browse. No search. No way to discover who lives in the building without a mutual connection.

⟳
Double opt-in only

Introductions only move forward with both parties saying yes. Names and contact details are never shared until both residents have accepted. Declining is private and never communicated.

▣
Building-scoped by default

For the Chorus pilot, all data is scoped to the building. Residents can see that their information is used only to serve connections within their community — not shared across properties or sold.

✦
Explicit consent flow

Every resident signs a plain-English privacy agreement during onboarding. What we collect, how we use it, and how to delete it — explained without legal jargon, revisable any time in Settings.

⊛
Manager oversight, not access

Building managers see community health metrics — connection rates, event engagement, satisfaction trends — but never individual conversation content, introduction responses, or resident preferences.

⤫
Trust & Safety queue

Residents can report inappropriate behavior at any time. Reports go to a manager review queue with a clear resolution process. Blocking is immediate and private.
