How Orbiit works
1. Check your matches
You’ll be notified by email when you and another user like each other.

2. Schedule your date or meetup
Share your availability and optionally your phone number, which will be revealed one hour before the date or meetup.

3. Receive your details
We’ll send you the final venue and time.

4. Need to reschedule?
Each user has up to two rescheduling opportunities.

5. Share your feedback
After each date or meetup, we highly recommend sharing feedback with us. This helps us improve Orbiit and get you even better matches and venues.

6. No ghosting policy
If you ghost without rescheduling, you will be removed from the platform.

Why we don’t allow chatting
We believe too much texting leads to fewer real-life meetups. Orbiit prioritizes meeting in person so genuine connections can form.
