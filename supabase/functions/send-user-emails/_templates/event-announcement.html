What’s new on Orbiit

We’ve rolled out our friendship survey and released a new version of Orbiit with several updates:

  • You can now delete your account at any time
  • You can change your survey responses at any time
  • You may add your phone number, which will be shared with your date one hour before the date
  • We’ll soon begin sending text message reminders to confirm dates. Until then, please continue confirming via email
  • You can report accounts that exhibit inappropriate behavior

Friendship survey update

After completing a short friendship survey, you’ll be matched and sent to a venue the same way Orbiit dating works, just tailored for friendships.

Please make sure to check and respond to your emails. If your time slot is not confirmed, the match will be released and you’ll be able to rematch later.

If you don’t receive new matches right away, don’t worry. We focus on quality over quantity to make sure matches are intentional and well aligned.

How Orbiit works

1. Check your matches You’ll be notified by email when you and another user like each other.
2. Schedule your date or meetup Share your availability and optionally your phone number, which will be revealed one hour before the date or meetup.
3. Receive your details We’ll send you the final venue and time.
4. Need to reschedule? Each user has up to two rescheduling opportunities.
5. Share your feedback After each date or meetup, we highly recommend sharing feedback with us. This helps us improve Orbiit and get you even better matches and venues.
6. No ghosting policy If you ghost without rescheduling, you will be removed from the platform.

Why we don’t allow chatting

We believe too much texting leads to fewer real-life meetups. Orbiit prioritizes meeting in person so genuine connections can form.
